How It Works

From brief to delivery in four steps

1

Tell us what you need

Fill out our project form with your vision, quantities, and timeline. Share brand guidelines, inspiration, or just a rough idea. We'll work from there.

2

Design & sampling

We'll create designs and produce physical samples for your approval. Refine until it's exactly right. You don't pay for production until you're happy.

3

Production

Your order goes into production at our manufacturing facility. Every piece is cut and sewn from scratch with premium materials and construction.

4

Delivery

Finished goods shipped directly to you, your office, or distributed to team members. We handle the logistics so you don't have to.

FAQ

Common questions

Our minimum project size is $2,500. This typically translates to around 50-100 pieces depending on the product type and customization level. For smaller quantities, reach out and we'll see if we can help or point you in the right direction.

We cut and sew everything from scratch. Unlike most corporate apparel companies that print or embroider on pre-made blanks, we manufacture the garments themselves. You choose the fabric, the weight, the colors, the cut, the construction details. It's genuinely custom, not just customized.

Standard production is 6-8 weeks from sample approval. This includes cutting, sewing, finishing, and quality control. Rush options are available for some products. Complex projects or very large orders may take longer. We'll give you a realistic timeline upfront.

Yes. If you have brand guidelines, we can work from those. If you have rough ideas, we can develop them. If you just want something that looks good, we can handle that too. Design support is included in our service. You're not expected to show up with finished artwork.

Premium. We use the same construction techniques and materials as retail apparel brands. This isn't promotional merchandise quality. The point is to make apparel your team actually wants to wear, which means it needs to look and feel like something they'd buy themselves.

Yes. We can ship bulk to your office, distribute to individual team members, or any combination. We handle the logistics. For distributed teams, we can set up individual shipments to home addresses.

Before production starts, changes are usually possible. Once cutting and sewing begins, it's harder. We'll work with you to accommodate reasonable changes, but the earlier you flag something, the easier it is to adjust. Communication is key.

Absolutely. Many of our clients order quarterly or annually. Once we've made something for you, reorders are straightforward. We keep your specs on file. Just tell us quantities and sizes, and we'll produce another run.
